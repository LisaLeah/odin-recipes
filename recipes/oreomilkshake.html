Oreo Milkshake Recipe

How To Make an Oreo Milkshake

Homemade milkshakes may take extra time to prepare but choosing to use this recipe than odering out may just prove to be one of your best ideas decisions.

The problem with most milkshakes is that most either are not thick enough, does not contain enough cookie crumbs or if you find one that ticks all the boxes it normally costs the price of a house (Not literally but you know what I mean).

  • Half pack Oreo Cookies
  • 3 Large Scoops Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 300ml Cold Milk
  • Extra Oreo Cookies to serve (optional)
  • Chocolate sauce to serve (optional)
  • Whipped or spray cream to serve

Steps to Success

  1. Add Oreos, milk and ice cream into a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour some chocolate sauce around the inside of your glass (optional). Pour milkshake into the glass and top with whipped cream, more chocolate sauce, crushed Oreos and a whole Oreo cookie.
